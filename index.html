Like Photo USA was founded on a simple principle: beauty without edge is boring. We reject the over-processed, plastic aesthetic of modern commercial photography in favor of raw texture, harsh shadows, and undeniable truth.

Led by creative director Julian Vance, our studio operates at the intersection of high fashion and street reality. We have collaborated with avant-garde designers, independent musicians, and global brands looking to shed their corporate skin.

Our process is analog at heart, even when digital in execution. We believe in getting it right in the camera, in the moment of tension between the lens and the subject.
