Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prakash Tours?
Prakash Tours is a premium car rental and travel service based in New Delhi, providing luxury and comfort vehicles with professional drivers for safe and stress-free journeys.
What kind of vehicles do you offer?
We offer a diverse fleet ranging from comfort sedans like the Maruti Suzuki Dzire, to luxury SUVs like the Toyota Innova Crysta and Kia Seltos, all the way up to premium luxury cars like the Mercedes-Benz E-Class.
Are there any hidden charges?
No. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. The quote you receive includes all basic charges. Tolls and parking are typically handled as per actuals during the trip unless a specific all-inclusive package is booked.
How do I book a car?
Simply select your dates and desired car on our website, click "Book Now", and you'll be redirected to our WhatsApp to instantly confirm your booking with our team.
Are your drivers experienced?
Yes, all our drivers are highly professional, polite, verified, and well-acquainted with the routes in and around New Delhi to ensure a smooth journey.
Where are you located?
Our office is located at floor, G, Lower Ground, 27, Bodofa Upendra Nath Brahma Rd, sector 4, Greater Kailash, New Delhi, Delhi 110048. You can find directions via Google Maps right above this section.
